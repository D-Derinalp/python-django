About Us

Global warming is a challenge that urgently requires the active participation of all civil society actors. We are a community that wants to raise awareness of climate change among societies and show possible scenarios to people by explaining the causes and consequences.

Here you will find detailed information about the causes and consequences of climate change. In addition, we will try to learn the public perspectives on climate change with a short survey we would like to do with you. We look forward to your assistance in this matter.
